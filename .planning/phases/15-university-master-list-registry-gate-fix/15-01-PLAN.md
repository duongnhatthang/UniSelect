Fix the registry gate that silently skips 95% of adapters, and migrate scrapers.json to the new schema with explicit website_url/scrape_url separation.

Purpose: The current `static_verified` boolean gate prevents all but 4 adapters from running. Replacing it with a `scrape_url` presence check means any adapter with a known cutoff page URL will run, while entries awaiting discovery stay inactive.

Output: Updated registry.ts, migrated scrapers.json, updated run.ts warning, and registry unit tests.
